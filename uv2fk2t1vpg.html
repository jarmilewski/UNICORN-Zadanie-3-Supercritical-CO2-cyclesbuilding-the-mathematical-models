Bae et al. compared various cycle layouts presented in Fig. \ref{297364} . The results showed that all of the analyzed sCO2 Brayton cycle layouts perform better than than the air Brayton cycle \citep{Grzebielec2017OPENENGINEERING7}, in particular the recompression Brayton, the cascading Brayton and the Rankine cycles can increase net hybrid system efficiency by over 10% more than the single MCFC system. \citep{Bae2014608} The aim of this paper is development of models of main elements of supercritical CO2 cycles. A typical CO2 based cycle is presented in Fig. \ref{297364}. There can be found the following element in each system:
  1. Compressor
  2. Expander (turbine)
  3. Heat Exchanger
  4. Pump (in Rankine cycles)
