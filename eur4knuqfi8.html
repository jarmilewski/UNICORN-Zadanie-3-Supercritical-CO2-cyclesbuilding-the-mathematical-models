It can be applied to water (incompressible flow, hydraulic turbine), to steam or any other gases as defined by stream type Gas, Fluegas, Universial Fluid, and 2-phase vapor/liquid ( compressible flow, turbo machinery). As such it is a most versatile component in EBSILON to model energy conversion by means of expansion of a process stream. Component General Expander represents a single expansion stage, a stage group, or a complete expansion section of the modeled equipment.
