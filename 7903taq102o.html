The heat exchanger is constructed of 316 stainless steel and it was designed to transfer 2.3 MW at a flow rate of 5.7 kg/s with a hot-side inlet temperature of 755 K (900°F) and a maximum allowable working pressure (MAWP) of 17.2 MPa  (Pasch 2012) .
